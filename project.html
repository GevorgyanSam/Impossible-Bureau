Project Pie

Pizza Gone Digital

Custom pizzas that don't suck.

Working with TDA Boulder we developed a dynamic site nearly as dynamic as this exciting concept pizza restaurant chain coming out of San Diego and spreading around the globe. There's a reason it's growing, and though we'd like to think it's due to our awesome execution of TDA's design, it's also likely due to the fact that the pizzas themselves are just plain awesome (and not too plain either).

A website as personal as pizza

The designers and good friends of ours over at TDA Boulder infused the site design with hand crafted touches like a custom font consisting of hand-drawn letters, and animations that felt drawn on paper. We took this into development and brought this unique style to life with subtle details in animation that let the site really stand out.

Inspired toppings all around you

Custom pizza you design is all about making your mark, and though you can't draw on the walls yourself, Project Pie has a wall of inspirational quotes that are hand written. You might just come across a saying or proverb that'll inspire your next pie and we took that concept digital.

The technologies we used

Behind the site is a solid Django CMS that allows for all content and information on the site to be updated. We love Django and our clients tend to as well. The site interacts with a wide range of services for things like online ordering, social media integration (instagram, facebook, twitter), and more.

Site Motion

Check out this video and you'll see we used a number of fun effects to illustrate the animated drawings, text, and hand-drawn feel of the site. Pizzas spin, get pressed, and get topped as you go through each section. The site is fully responsive across desktop, mobile, and tablet so you should have fun no matter what devices you're on.
