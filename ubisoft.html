Watch Dogs: CTOS Chicago

A city controlled by an operating system.

Working with Ignition in Los Angeles, we developed an interactive simulation of what a city totally controlled by a sophisticated all-knowing central operating system would be like and well, how it would be sold to a security seeking public unaware of the terrible side effects depicted in Ubisoft’s hit game “Watch Dogs”.

The site scrolls through various interactive presentations of the benefits of controlling a city through an omnipresent operating system. The narration delivered through HTML5 coincides with various interactive animations and videos of a model of Chicago.

Check out the site here or watch our screencast with audio.
