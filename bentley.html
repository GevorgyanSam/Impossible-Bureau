Bentley

The New Flying Spur

The Power to perform

As users scroll through the site the performance section illustrated the impressive acccelleration of the New Flying Spur by illustrating the distribution of torque between the front and rear axles using HTML5 canvas.

A cocoon of luxury

By sliding through several impressive photos of the interior of the New Flying Spur the site illustrated the luxurious interior and highlighted some of the details like the stitching, wood accents, and luxury features.

We paid close attention to the performance and feel of the transitions on the site to help ensure that the experience of the car was matched by the experience of the site, silky smooth animation and soft subtle transitions.

Luxury and presence

In several sections of the site we made sure the cropping and sizing adjusted well to every desktop screensize users might be visiting the site on, framing gorgeous assets and illustrations of the car as they transitioned vertically and horizontally through the experience.

The connected car

Focussing on some of the more advanced technical features the site walks through several of them and highlights the internal phone, navigation, back-seat screens, and amazing audio.

The highlights are available in focussed lightbox sections with galleries users can go through to see each element in detail and materials forreading and specs are available as well.
