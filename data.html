Data is coming

Our latest work in progress

At Impossible Bureau we’ve always loved working on projects that are as beautiful as they are useful and interesting. We’re getting pretty excited about a project that we’re working on right now that is both, and it’s just a sneak peek at something even bigger we’ve been thinking about and working on for over a year now.

This project has been a fun challenge for the whole team. We’ve been creating and structuring a huge amount of data, as well as experimenting with how far we can go with sentiment analysis, meteor, and canvas. We’ve broken it all down and gathered all sorts of facts about the types of words being used, number of lines per character, average interaction rates, and we’ve even started comparing it all to volume levels in each episode.

See an early sneak peek of some of the designs on dribbble. We’re still busy coding and excited to get it out for everyone to see next month!
