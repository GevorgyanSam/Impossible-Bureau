Meet Julia

Director of Production & Strategy

Originally from Sydney, Australia, Julia now heads up production and strategy from our SLC office after a 4-year stopover in New York. She keeps our projects humming and makes sure that the end product delivers on the business goals and resonates with the intended audience. Here's some lesser-known information about our Director of Production & Strategy.

What made you want to be an interactive producer?

I've always felt a need to create things, and started out my career as a web developer when I discovered that I loved building websites. I never planned to be a producer - I didn't even know what a producer was - until a former boss called me up and asked me to be his producer at a small interactive design agency. As a producer, I really enjoyed being involved at every step of the process and helping products and campaigns come together perfectly through planning, design and development.

What is currently on your desk?

Headphones, post-it notes, RFP, almonds.

What is the best decision you've made?

To not look too far ahead, and let my life and career take me in a direction I would never have planned.

As it relates to our industry, what are you most excited about?

I'm excited that we're constantly finding new ways to communicate and tell stories. I'm also excited about digital products we can build beyond the traditional screen, particularly wearables.
